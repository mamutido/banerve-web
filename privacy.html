Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 2026 · Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how BANERVE ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our advertising platform at mamutido.github.io/banerve-web.

1. Information We Collect

Account information: When you register, we collect your email address and, if you sign in via Google, your name and profile picture provided by Google OAuth.

Financial information: We collect cryptocurrency wallet addresses you provide for withdrawal purposes. We do not store private keys or payment credentials.

Usage data: We collect standard server logs including IP addresses, browser type, and pages visited to maintain service security and performance.

Transaction data: Records of deposits and withdrawals processed through our platform are stored for accounting and audit purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to: operate and maintain the platform; process payments and withdrawals; verify your identity and prevent fraud; send transactional emails (deposit confirmations, withdrawal codes); and comply with applicable legal obligations.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

Supabase: We use Supabase for database hosting and authentication. Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure. See supabase.com/privacy.

CryptoCloud: Payment processing is handled by CryptoCloud. Transaction data is shared with CryptoCloud as necessary to process payments. See cryptocloud.plus.

Google: If you use Google Sign-In, Google's privacy policy applies to that authentication process. See policies.google.com/privacy.

4. Data Retention

Account data is retained for the duration of your account. Transaction records are retained for a minimum of 5 years for audit purposes. You may request deletion of your account by contacting us.

5. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS), row-level security on our database, and restricted access to sensitive operations. No system is 100% secure; use the platform at your own risk.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Cookies

We use localStorage to store your session token locally. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
